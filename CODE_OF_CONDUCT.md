# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We as members, contributors, and leaders commit to making participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

Standards
- Be respectful and considerate in interactions.
- Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior.
- Be mindful of different viewpoints and experiences.

Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers at [MAINTAINER_EMAIL]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate.

For full details, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
